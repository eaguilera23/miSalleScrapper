What happened with the app?

In mid february, while searching how to access the information of the Universidad De La Salle Bajío students, a vulnerability was found, which permits the access to all the information, to anyone with an internet conection. At August 14th miSalle was launched in the Google Play Store, showing the registered student its schedule, grades, credits and the next payment due date. Information that previously was behind of more than 5 clicks, 2 websites and 1 or 2 forms. With the vulnerability in consideration, I protected the students information by checking their username and password, both expended by the university.

miSalle reached the 4% of its population target in less than 24 hrs. Then it was blocked by the institution on August 15th around 2 pm. The motive that the school authorities gave to me was that "the school already has monetary agreements with a software company and a bank" and "in 3 weeks the school will launch an app". This promised app was supposed to launch on September 4th of 2017, but nothing has been announced or launched yet.
